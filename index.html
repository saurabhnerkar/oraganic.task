Organic Farming Research Foundation works to foster the improvement and widespread adoption of organic farming systems. OFRF cultivates organic research, education, and federal policies that bring more farmers and acreage into organic production.

collaboration

OFRF Urges NOSB to Keep
Driving Bold Farmer-Centered
Organic Research
May 1st, 2025|News

OFRF submitted comments and delivered
oral testimony at the National Organic
Standards Board's spring meeting urging
continued investment in bold, farmer-centered
organic research priorities. Our recommendations
address persistent gaps and propose new focus areas
to help the organic sector thrive.
